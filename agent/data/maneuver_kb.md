## About Maneuver

Maneuver is a product studio that partners with startups and scale-ups to design, build, 
and ship software products. Founded in 2022, Maneuver has helped over 40 companies go from 
idea to launched product.

We're not a typical dev shop that takes a spec and codes it. We're product partners — we 
help founders figure out what to build, not just how to build it.

## Services

### Product Strategy & Discovery
We run intensive discovery sprints to validate ideas before writing a single line of code. 
This includes user research, competitive analysis, market sizing, and feature prioritization. 
Typical engagement: 2-3 week sprint.

### UI/UX Design
Full-service product design from wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes. We design for 
real users, not awards. Every design decision is backed by user insight. Our design team 
works in Figma and ships design systems, not just mockups.

### Full-Stack Engineering
We build web and mobile applications using modern stacks: React, Next.js, React Native, 
Node.js, Python, and cloud-native infrastructure on AWS/GCP. We write clean, tested, 
production-grade code.

### AI & Machine Learning Integration
We help companies integrate AI into their products — from LLM-powered features to custom 
ML models. We've built conversational AI, recommendation engines, intelligent search, 
and data pipelines.

### Growth & Launch Support
We don't just build and disappear. We help with launch strategy, analytics setup, 
A/B testing frameworks, and the first 90 days post-launch.

## Our Process

### 1. Discovery Call (Free)
A 30-minute conversation to understand your vision, challenges, and goals. This is where 
we figure out if we're the right fit for each other.

### 2. Proposal & Scoping
Within 48 hours of the discovery call, we send a detailed proposal with scope, timeline, 
team composition, and pricing. No surprises.

### 3. Kickoff & Design Sprint
We start with a focused design sprint (1-2 weeks) to nail down the user experience 
and technical architecture before any development begins.

### 4. Build & Iterate
Agile development in 2-week sprints. You get demos every sprint, and we adjust based 
on your feedback. Transparency is non-negotiable.

### 5. Launch & Handoff
We handle deployment, monitoring setup, and documentation. We do a proper knowledge 
transfer so your team (or your next team) can take over confidently.

## Pricing Model

We offer two engagement models:

### Project-Based
Fixed scope, fixed price. Best for well-defined projects with clear deliverables. 
Typical range: $15K - $150K depending on complexity. Most MVPs land in the $25K-$60K range.

### Monthly Retainer
Dedicated team members working on your product continuously. Best for ongoing development 
and products that need continuous iteration. Starts at $12K/month for a small team 
(designer + 2 engineers).

We're transparent about pricing from day one. No hidden fees, no scope creep surprises.

## FAQ

**How quickly can you start?**
Typically within 1-2 weeks of signing. We maintain bench capacity for quick starts.

**Do you work with non-technical founders?**
Absolutely. Many of our best partnerships are with founders who have deep domain expertise 
but need a technical partner to bring their vision to life.

**Can you work with our existing team?**
Yes. We can augment your team, work alongside them, or take full ownership of a workstream.

**What if the project scope changes?**
It always does. We build in flexibility with regular check-ins and a change management 
process that keeps everyone aligned without slowing things down.

**Do you sign NDAs?**
Yes, happy to sign mutual NDAs before any detailed discussions.
